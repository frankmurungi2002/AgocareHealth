My 3-year-old child developed a heat rash that won't go away

We live in Bushenyi and it has been very hot lately. The rash is mostly on the neck and back. We tried cool baths but it persists. Is this dangerous?

Dr. Sarah Nabirye ✓
Pediatrician • Mulago Hospital

Heat rash (miliaria) is common in hot climates. Keep the area dry and cool. Avoid applying heavy creams. If it develops pus or fever, visit a clinic immediately. Calamine lotion can help soothe itching.

Is it safe to exercise during the third trimester?

I'm currently 8 months pregnant and have been doing light walking and yoga. My OB said it's fine, but I'm worried about the baby. What type of exercise is safest at this stage?

Dr. Victoria Mukasa ✓
Obstetrician • Kampala Women's Hospital

Light exercise like walking, swimming, and prenatal yoga is beneficial during the third trimester. Avoid high-impact activities and heavy lifting. Always listen to your body and stop if you feel dizzy or short of breath. Regular exercise helps with labor and postpartum recovery.

Red eyes and itching - could it be conjunctivitis?

For the past 2 days, both my eyes have been red, itchy, and producing a yellow discharge. A colleague at work also has the same symptoms. Is this contagious and how can I treat it at home?

Dr. Hassan Mwangi ✓
Eye Specialist • Nairobi Eye Center

Yellow discharge suggests bacterial conjunctivitis, which is highly contagious. Avoid touching your eyes and wash your hands frequently. Seek medical attention for antibiotic eye drops. Do not share towels or eye cosmetics with others.

Mental Health 1 hour ago

How do I manage anxiety and panic attacks?

I've been experiencing sudden panic attacks with chest tightness and shortness of breath. They happen without warning and make me feel like something is seriously wrong. How can I manage these episodes?

Dr. David Kariuki ✓
Psychiatrist • Mental Health Center

Panic attacks are treatable. During an attack: breathe slowly (4 in, 6 out), ground yourself using the 5-4-3-2-1 technique, and reassure yourself it will pass. Long-term management includes CBT therapy and sometimes medication. Speaking with a mental health professional is crucial for proper diagnosis and treatment plan.
